Young Athletes Program

Company Name: Young Athletes Program

From: August 2015 To: December 2015

Description of Position: Set up games for the children to participate in.

Reflection: I participated in the Young Athletes Program when I was in college at Northwest Missouri State University. I helped set up games and activities for children with disabilities to participate in. During this experience, I learned how to modify games so that children with disabilities can participate. This knowledge would be helpful during a PE class that has a child with a physical disability.




Student Teaching

Company Name: Park Hill School District

From: January 2018 To: April 2018

Description of Position: Shadow a teacher and eventually co-teach with that teacher.

Reflection: I student taught in an elementary school in a special education classroom for 12 weeks and a 3rd grade classroom for 4 weeks. During this time, I was observing the classroom teacher and learning how to prepare lessons and classroom management techniques. I also co-taught with the teacher to learn better instruction techniques and learn how to differentiate my instruction.




Summer School Teacher

Company Name: Park Hill School District

From: June 2018 To: July 2018

Description of Position: Follow lesson plans prepared by the district to teach a class of second graders.

Reflection: During this job I taught a second grade class. This was my first real teaching experience outside of student teaching. I learned so much during this experience. I learned how I wanted to set up my classroom, techniques to deliver lessons, classroom management strategies, and how to collaborate with teachers. If I were to do anything different during this job, I would have had a better classroom management technique. I would have had my students help me set up the classroom rules.
